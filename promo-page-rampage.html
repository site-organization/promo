  • Puck drops at 7:30PM
  • The San Antonio Rampage will celebrate breast cancer survivors and raise awareness for breast health during their 9th annual Pink in the Rink game.

March 1, 2019: Military Appreciation

  • Puck drops at 7:30PM
  • The San Antonio Rampage will honor both former and active duty armed forces with their 16th annual Military Appreciation Night.

  • Puck drops at 3:00PM

  • Fans are encouraged to bring their four-legged friend to the game to cheer on the Rampage. Dog tickets can be purchased with a human ticket for an additional $5.

April 6, 2019: First Responders Night

  • Puck drops at 7PM
